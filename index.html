We are creating a future where students are rewarded in the pursuit of their career based on their abilities and experience.

As any recent grad can tell you, finding your first internship or first job after graduating college is hard. Without relevant job experience, it can be difficult to demonstrate your skills to a potential employer.

Leadership as experience

Far far away, Students volunteering activities have been of interest to academics, but the impact of these activities on learning and employability has been neglected. Through our platform students can present that category of experience in a number of ways and get rewarded with opportunities form companies.

Project based hiring

Rather than filling out resumes, candidates create online project-based profiles by performing simulated tasks that they will be doing on the actual jobs. Employers will then select their favorite candidates to bring in for interviews.

Right to opportunity
